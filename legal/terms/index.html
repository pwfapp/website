4. What the Service does

Proud Man Plan helps you set a personal goal — a "mission" your 12-year-old self would be proud of — and work towards it one small action ("step") at a time. The central idea is that you write to your 12-year-old self at meaningful moments, as a way to keep what matters in view.

An AI coach (powered by Anthropic's Claude model) is used at four points in the flow:

  • during onboarding, to learn what typically gets in the way of you following through
  • when you are choosing your mission, to help you pick something specific and meaningful
  • when you are choosing each weekly step, to help you make it concrete, realistic, and due within a week
  • when you check in on a step — either generating a short letter from your present self to your 12-year-old self if you completed it, or helping you decide whether to redo, shrink, or pause the step if you didn't

A second letter is generated when you mark a whole mission complete, looking back across the journey.

You decide what missions and steps to pursue. The AI coach makes suggestions and writes the letters; you choose what to act on.
